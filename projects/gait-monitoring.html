Decisionics Lab – Gait Monitoring Research

Decisionics Lab | 2023

Project Overview

  • Developed a low-cost wearable gait monitoring device designed for real-world deployment and continuous use.
  • Focused on hardware validation, signal processing, and data optimization to improve robustness and accuracy.

My Role

  • Terrain Testing: Designed and conducted an obstacle-based evaluation protocol across 12 different terrains, including stairs and uneven surfaces, to validate robustness under realistic walking conditions.
  • Signal Processing: Applied filtering and preprocessing techniques to address sensor noise and inconsistencies, improving the reliability of extracted gait events.
  • Data Integration: Refactored Python-based data processing pipelines, reducing computation time by 30%. Integrated SQLite3 for structured data storage and efficient retrieval.

Outcome & Impact

  • Achieved approximately 5% error margin in temporal gait parameters, significantly improving accuracy compared to existing low-cost commercial systems.
  • Ensured reliable device performance across all tested terrains, supporting robust real-world use.
  • Improved data integrity and sensor reliability through advanced filtering and structured data management.
